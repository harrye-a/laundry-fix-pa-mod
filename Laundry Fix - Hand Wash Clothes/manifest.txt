Name                "Laundry Fix - Hand Wash Clothes"
Author              "Q9E"
Description         "I got fed up with the broken Laundry rooms, baskets, 
and other sillines that meant the clothing need wasn't being fulfilled.
\r\nThis mod aims to fix that, by allowing prisoners to reduce their own clothing need
by hand-washing their clothes at a sink and mirror, bathroom sink or shower head.
\r\n I have no idea if this mod is compatible with others, this mod was produced out of sheer annoyance,
and I considered just disabling clothing needs altogether, but hopefully this adds a more 'in-world' solution."
